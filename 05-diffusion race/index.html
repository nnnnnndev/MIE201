Vacancy vs. Interstitial Track
Interstitial Diffusivity (Outer):
Orders of magnitude faster. Empty adjacent sites are always available.

Vacancy Diffusivity (Inner):
Severely bottlenecked. The tracer must wait for a rare vacancy to randomly drift into an adjacent site before it can move.
